Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the MotoBaana app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices. It was created by Heikki Turja (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as a Free service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

Information Collection and Use

The Application collects information when you download and use it. This may include:

  • Your device's Internet Protocol address (IP address).
  • The pages of the Application that you visit, the time and date of your visit, and time spent on the Application.
  • The operating system version used on your mobile device.

Location Data

The Application accesses your device's location to provide map-viewing features. This location data is processed locally on your device to show your position on the map and is not stored on the Service Provider's servers. Specifically:

  • Geolocation Services: Used solely to display the user's current position on the map.
  • Ephemeral Processing: Data is used in real-time and is not retained after the purpose is served.

Artificial Intelligence (AI)

The Application does not use Artificial Intelligence (AI) technologies to process user data or provide interactive AI features to the end-user.
